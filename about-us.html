Creators

Tanya W.

Tanya was born in Korea and has moved all over the states since a young age. She is a recent graduate from the University of Washington with a degree in Psychology, a proud mother of two dogs, and a passionate gamer. She currently works at Microsoft as a user research coordinator under the Xbox team and hopes to one day work as a UX Researcher or a UX designer.

Hannah S.

Hannah was born and raised in Seattle. She graduated from the University of Washington with a degree in linguistics and Japanese, and is now studying software development at Code Fellows. In her off time, she enjoys reading technical books, playing video games, hiking, bouldering, and playing with her five pet parrots and dog.

Matt M.

Matt was born and raised in Hawaii, attending the University of Hawaii where he earned a B.S in Kinesiology and Rehab Science. Wanting to pursue his doctorate, he moved to Seattle and started working at a physical therapy clinic in Bellevue. After working in close proximity to the tech industry, he chose to pursue a career in software development.
